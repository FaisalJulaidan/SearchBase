Billing support

Monthly Payments

We charge your account every 28 days. If you cancel your subscription, you will be able to use the services until the end of the month. Your account will not be deleted unless you request for your account to be deleted however your acess will be downgraded to the Free version of the software.

The database(s) and products that you have added, will not be deleted in case you wish to upgrade again


Your Data

In order to process your payemnts with our payment service provider (Stripe) we will need basic forms of payments information to process your payment. We will not store this information as our payment service provider will retain this information for recuring payments.
