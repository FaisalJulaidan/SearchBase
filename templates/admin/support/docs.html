Getting to know our platform

Traffic Monitor your traffic

Using our platform you can access all of the traffic that you are receiving on you assistant. All of the data that you are receiving is directly from user interaction and not been modified or changed.

All of the data that you receive, can be exported to CSV or Excel formats. We are working on making more file types, but if you need a specific file type, we can produce the data for you.

Analytics Data about your users

The data produced from analytics, will show you what questions users are answering, how much time they are spending on the assistant and finally what products are being returned to the user.

This data can also be extracted to certain file formats such as CSV or Excel formats.
