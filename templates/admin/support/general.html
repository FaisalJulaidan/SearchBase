FAQ

A few questions you may have

How does my hosting work?

We host all of your data on our servers. All of the information that is sensitive will be encrypted to ensure it cannot be accessed

Can others access my assistant?

No, we encrypt all of your data so others cannot access your assistant

Can I export my analytics?

Yes, you can do this by clicking the 'Export' button on the top left of your analytics page

Can I change the design of my assistant?

Yes, you will have to let us know as we currently are working on technologies to enable this process for users to make changes directly.

I want certain features for my assistant?

We are looking for valuable feedback. You can contact our team and set the subject line to 'R&D' and we will look at making the feature available to you as soon as possible.
