Adding your Questions


For you to get set up, you must first register the questions that you would like to ask your visitors.

To enable your users to make the most use of the assistant, ask specific questions that quickly understands what it is they're looking for or what they need.

Make sure to ask questions that can enable you to steer users to products on your website or information that you want to display.

Once you have bundled your questions, start to enter how many questions you wish to ask and enter your questions.


Adding your Answers


Once you have entered your questions, you can start entering your answers for those answers

In order for you to correctly control your visitors, you must set answers that relate to your questions, but more importantly can lead to the next question. This gives you valuable data to understand what the user wants.

You can enter text, an icon and keywords for your answers. The keywords will have the biggest impact. The text in to inform the user of what they are selecting and the image is to show a visual demonstration of the product.

However the Keyword will be the route that searches your products for in the database that you create.


Adding your products


The final step of creating the intelligence for your assistant, is to add the products that you wish to present to the user.

These can be products that you are selling on your website or, products that you simply wish to suggest to users.

To add these products, you must first figure an ID, Name, Brand (optional), Model (Optional), Price (optional) and Keywords You can later add a discount percentage, a URL to take the user to a specific page and an image.

Here the keyword is the most important piece of data. The keywords from your answer's keywords should match your products, in order for the system for retrieve them.


Importing your products

You can also upload your own products database by uploading an XML and JSON files. We're working on developing more file upload solutions.

By pressing the 'Upload File' button at the top the Products page, it will ask you to select the file. Once the file has been uploaded, your products will then be added to the table on the products page

Emojis are fun. Can I use them?

Yes! If you want to use emojis on your questions, answers or products simply write the emoji in textbox with them.

The emoji should be written in their "short form", meaning for example :smile: or :bed:. Users can also enter emojis including through their native phone.
