Deploying your assistant



In the side menu on the left of your screen, you should first open the 'Connection' page.

This page will have some pre-made HTMl, CSS and Javascript code made for you. You must paste the lines of your code into your HTML page. If you wish to deploy your assistant on a certain page, the paste the code on the relevant page.

Where is says 'nameofyourcompany' after our domain, you must enter your company name. Else you can visit your profile page and click on 'Assistant' which will load your assistant on full screen.

You can the copy that URL and replace it with the line starting with 'www.thesearchbase.com/nameofyourcompany'

If you are struggling with any of the steps above, please contact our team and we'll be happy to help
