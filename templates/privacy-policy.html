Privacy Policy

How we use your data.


Giving our users the best experience, therefore, we take matters such as privacy very seriously. We apply our privacy policy across TheSearchBase's ecosystem. If you do not agree with our policy you may not use our services.


Personal information

In order for you to make functional use of our services, we will need personal and company information. If you wish for your details not be shared with us where possible, you may skip the submission. However, in required fields, you must provide us with your information.

If you do cancel your account with us, we will delete all of your information promptly and securely that we hold about you. However, we still hold the right to keep any Anonymous Data collected and continue to further use this information.


Sharing your data

We may share your information with trusted suppliers that we continuously engage with to help us provide certain information in order to help us provide certain functionality such as handling payments and subscriptions. We will use your data in reasonable endeavours to control and be responsible for the use of your information by such suppliers.


Keeping your personal Information secure

The security of your personal/company information is our priority and we have both technical and physical procedures to keep your data secure at all times to help prevent any unauthorised access, use or manipulation as Law in England. Once your data has been received by us, we will run technical procedures and security features to try to prevent unauthorised access including:

  • by encrypting any Personal/Company Information which we transfer to Stripe

  • Only use the information necessary to provide you the platform

Changes to our Privacy Policy

In the instance that we do make changes to this policy, we will post the revised policy here with an updated message and a further email will be sent to inform you of the changes.
